Edward S. Hansen

Edward Hansen is a lighting designer, artist, and visual storyteller working in theatre, installations, and live events. He has a passion for musical theater and is experienced with everything from opera to black box new work theatre and dance performance. He uses his unique visual vocabulary to design immersive and evocative lighting environments to enhance and support the storytelling and tonal arc of a piece.

He received his B.F.A. in Theatre Design from the University of Southern California.
